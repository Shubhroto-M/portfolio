Vanguard 25

Designing a 5S 25A Battery Management System from the ground up.

The Challenge

Standard off-the-shelf BMS modules often lack the specific thermal dissipation characteristics and form-factor requirements needed for custom robotics or hybrid energy storage setups. The goal of Vanguard 25 was to design a robust 5-series (5S) lithium-ion battery management system capable of sustaining a continuous 25A draw without thermal throttling.

Architecture & Iteration

The core of the design revolves around balancing cell voltages while providing strict over-current, over-voltage, and under-voltage protection. During the initial schematic phase, trace width for the high-current paths was a major consideration.

Instead of relying purely on copper pours, I calculated the required widths and reinforced the critical paths to ensure the resistance remained low enough to prevent catastrophic heating under full load.
